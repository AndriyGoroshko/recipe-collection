  1. Prepare all the ingredients for juicy chicken shawarma. TM "Nasha Ryaba" chicken drumsticks come boneless, saving you time. Do not take the lavash out of its package ahead of time—unwrap it only right before using to prevent drying out. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Remove the skin from the four drumsticks, trim excess fat, and place in a baking dish. Crush 3 garlic cloves and add to the chicken. Season with salt and pepper. Add thyme leaves (fresh or dried), drizzle with 1.5 tbsp of oil, and bake for 25–30 minutes at 180°C.
  3. Meanwhile, make the sauce. In a bowl, add 200 g of plain yogurt. Finely chop the leaves from 7 parsley sprigs and mince 2 garlic cloves. Add the herbs and garlic to the yogurt along with ¼ tsp of fennel seeds. Season with salt and pepper and mix well.
  4. Remove the cooked chicken from the oven and let it cool slightly. Then dice the meat into small pieces.
  5. Cut 3 pickled cucumbers into long slices and then into thin strips. Slice one large tomato into thin half-moons and finely shred ¼ of a small cabbage.
  6. Spread the sauce over the lavash. Layer with cabbage, Korean-style carrots, tomato slices, and pickled cucumbers. Add the chicken, then drizzle with more sauce and ketchup.
  7. Fold the lavash like an envelope: tuck in the edges and roll into a wrap. Repeat the process with the remaining lavash sheets.
  8. Heat a dry grill pan and sear the shawarma for a few minutes on each side until grill marks form. Serve with extra sauce if desired.
